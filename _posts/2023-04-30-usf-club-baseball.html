--- layout: post title: "USF Club Baseball" date: 2023-04-30 ---

One of my motivations for enrolling in a part-time graduate program at USF was the opportunity to participate in club sports. Even though I only played a few years of little league baseball back in the day, I was excited to mix it up from my usual volleyball scene and try something new.

This experience was definitely outside of my comfort zone, and I couldn't help but think of the movie 22 Jump Street due to my age difference. The USF club baseball team was a really welcoming environment with great people. Getting to learn the basics again was a rewarding experience.
